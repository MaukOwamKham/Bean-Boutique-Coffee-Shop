Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my delivery date?

You can easily adjust your delivery schedule at any time through your account dashboard under the "Manage Subscription" tab.

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes, absolutely! If you are traveling or have too much coffee on hand, you can pause or resume your subscription with a single click in your settings.

Is there a cancellation fee?

Never. Our subscriptions are completely commitment-free. You can cancel your subscription anytime before your next billing cycle without any penalties.

When is the coffee roasted and shipped?

We roast in small batches every week. Your subscription box is packaged and dispatched within 24 hours of roasting to ensure ultimate aroma and freshness.
